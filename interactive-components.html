Accordion Component

An accordion is a UI component that collapses and expands sections of content, saving space on the page.
Accordions reduce cognitive load by showing only relevant content at a time, making pages easier to scan.
Use accordions for FAQs, settings panels, product details, or any content organized into logical sections.

Interaction Design Principles

Feedback

Immediate visual feedback for every user action. Users always know the result of their interaction.

Consistency

Similar actions behave similarly. Users build mental models and predict outcomes.

Discoverability

Interactive elements are obvious. Hover states, focus rings, and affordances guide users.

Efficiency

Micro-interactions are smooth and fast. Users stay in flow state.

Accessibility

All interactions work with keyboard, screen readers, and assistive technology.

Responsiveness

Touch targets are appropriately sized. Interactions work across all devices.
